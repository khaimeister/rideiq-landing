RideIQ

RideIQ is a cycling analytics app that connects with Strava to help riders track performance and improve over time.

Key Features

  • RideIQ Score – Overall ride performance metric based on your personal history
  • Efficiency Index – Measures how efficiently you ride
  • Fitness Score – Shows your recent performance trends
  • Engine Score – Tracks your top-end power improvements over time
